Plant Function

How a plant functions is connected to its plant structure. Plants are made out of plant cells which have a crucial and important role in carrying out the day-to-day processess and functions of plants. Its primary job is to perform photosynthesis which takes place in the plant cell's chloroplasts. Photosynthesis is the process by which plants use carbon dioxide, sunlight, and water to prepare food. In the process, ATP, or energy, is created. Also, plants have a lot of functions that aid in the balance nature and life. Plants generate oxygen (through photosynthesis), which is essential to life as well as serve as a source of food for sustenance. In addition, trees offer to animals as refuge and are recognized for their health advantages. In general, the parts of the plant have distinct functions to fulfill. In their own helpful way, plants sustain the ecological balance and serve as a source of sustenance (food and oxygen).
